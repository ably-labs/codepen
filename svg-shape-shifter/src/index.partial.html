Shape Shifting in realtime

Click anywhere to share a shape with connected viewers

The demo renders SVG shapes in realtime with low latency messaging. Simultaneous users can participate by exchanging shape coordinates, so share the link with a friend or open multiple browser tabs and watch the magic happen.

Each click generates a shape and sends its characteristics to all connected users. To achieve this, we send messages over an Ably realtime channel with "rewind" enabled, which replays the last few messages on an active channel.
